Explorer's Pack
Cost: 10 gp • Weight: 55 lbs
Contains: Backpack, Bedroll, 2 flasks of Oil, 10 days of Rations, Rope, Tinderbox, 10 Torches, and Waterskin.
Common Equipment
Backpack
Cost: 2 gp • Weight: 5 lbs
Holds up to 30 pounds within 1 cubic foot. Can also serve as a saddlebag.
Common Equipment
Bedroll
Cost: 1 gp • Weight: 7 lbs
Sleeps one Small or Medium creature. While in a Bedroll, you automatically succeed on saving throws against extreme cold.
Common Equipment
Oil
Cost: 1 sp • Weight: 1 lb
Dousing a Creature or Object: Attack action to throw within 20ft. Target must make DEX save (DC 8 + DEX mod + Prof) or be covered. Takes extra 5 Fire damage if hit with fire before oil dries (1 min).

Dousing a Space: Utilize action to cover 5ft square within 5ft. If lit, burns for 2 rounds dealing 5 Fire damage to creatures in area.

Fuel: Burns for 6 hours in Lamp/Lantern. Can be extinguished and relit.
Common Equipment
Rations
Cost: 5 sp • Weight: 2 lbs
Travel-ready food including jerky, dried fruit, hardtack, and nuts. Required to avoid malnutrition.
Common Equipment
Rope
Cost: 1 gp • Weight: 5 lbs
Tying: DC 10 DEX (Sleight of Hand) check as Utilize action
Breaking: DC 20 STR (Athletics) check
Binding: Only works on Grappled, Incapacitated, or Restrained creatures. Bound legs cause Restrained condition.
Escaping: DC 15 DEX (Acrobatics) check as an action
Common Equipment
Tinderbox
Cost: 5 sp • Weight: 1 lb
Contains flint, fire steel, and tinder for starting fires.

• Bonus Action to light Candle, Lamp, Lantern, or Torch
• 1 minute to light other fires
Common Equipment
Torch
Cost: 1 cp • Weight: 1 lb
Burns for 1 hour. Casts Bright Light in 20ft radius and Dim Light for additional 20ft.

Can be used as Simple Melee weapon: 1 Fire damage on hit.
Common Equipment
Waterskin
Cost: 2 sp • Weight: 5 lbs
Holds up to 4 pints of water. Required to avoid dehydration.
